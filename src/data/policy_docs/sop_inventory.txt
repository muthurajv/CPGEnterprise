Standard Operating Procedure: Inventory Management & Replenishment
Document: SOP-INV-003 | Revision: 5 | Effective: March 2026

PURPOSE
Define the process for monitoring inventory levels, triggering replenishment, and managing stockout risk across CPG Enterprise warehouses.

SCOPE
Applies to all warehouse operations: WH-EMEA-01, WH-EMEA-02, WH-AMER-01, WH-AMER-02, WH-APAC-01, WH-APAC-02

KEY METRICS AND THRESHOLDS
Reorder Point (ROP): Defined per SKU in inventory master. Triggers procurement review when stock_level falls to or below ROP.
Safety Stock: Minimum 30-day supply for standard SKUs; 90-day for baby/pharmaceutical.
Stockout Risk Zone: stock_level < 50% of ROP — immediate escalation required.
Critical Stockout: stock_level = 0 — P1 incident, customer service notification required within 2 hours.

REPLENISHMENT WORKFLOW
Step 1: AI system monitors stock levels every 4 hours via SAP inventory feed.
Step 2: When stock_level <= ROP, AI demand forecasting agent generates 90-day forward demand estimate.
Step 3: AI procurement agent selects optimal vendor and calculates order quantity.
Step 4: AI policy approval agent evaluates order against current thresholds.
Step 5: Auto-approved orders create PO in SAP automatically.
Step 6: Human-review orders route to Procurement Manager queue (SLA: 24 hours).
Step 7: All orders logged with AI trace_id for auditability.

DEMAND FORECASTING INPUTS
- 18 months of weekly shipment history per SKU
- Regional promotion calendar
- Seasonal adjustment factors (Q4 holiday +35%, summer +20% for beverages)
- Competitor stockout flags (when available)
- Economic indicator adjustments

ESCALATION MATRIX
Level 1 (Procurement Manager): stock_level < ROP for any SKU
Level 2 (Supply Chain Director): stock_level < 50% ROP for >5 SKUs in same warehouse
Level 3 (VP Operations): Warehouse-wide shortage affecting >20 SKUs
Level 4 (C-Suite): Customer-facing stockout with retailer penalty exposure

KPI TARGETS
Fill Rate: >= 98.5% (cases shipped / cases ordered)
Forecast Accuracy (MAPE): <= 8% for beverages, <= 12% for snacks
Inventory Turnover: >= 8x per year for FMCG, >= 4x for household
Days of Supply: 45-60 days standard, 30 days maximum for perishables
Stockout Rate: < 0.5% of SKU-warehouse combinations at any time

AI GOVERNANCE REQUIREMENTS
All AI recommendations must be logged with:
- model_version
- confidence_score
- top_reasons (min 3)
- trace_id (linked to OTel trace)
- human_review_required flag
Human acceptance rate of AI recommendations is tracked monthly.
Target: >85% acceptance rate; below 70% triggers model review.
