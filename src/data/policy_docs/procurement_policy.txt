CPG Enterprise Procurement Policy — Version 3.2
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. SCOPE
This policy governs all procurement activities for finished goods, raw materials, packaging, and indirect supplies across all CPG Enterprise regions (EMEA, AMER, APAC).

2. APPROVAL THRESHOLDS
2.1 Purchase Orders
- Up to $10,000: Auto-approved by AI procurement system
- $10,001 to $50,000: Requires Procurement Manager approval within 24 hours
- $50,001 to $250,000: Requires VP Supply Chain + Finance Director approval within 48 hours
- Over $250,000: Requires C-Suite approval (CFO + COO) within 5 business days

2.2 Emergency Procurement
In cases of stockout risk (stock_level < 20% of reorder_point), approval thresholds are doubled and turnaround time is halved.

3. VENDOR SELECTION CRITERIA
3.1 Approved Vendor List
All vendors must appear on the Approved Vendor List (AVL). New vendors require a 6-week qualification process.

3.2 Selection Priorities (in order)
a) Reliability score >= 0.90 required
b) Lead time must not exceed category maximum (see Appendix A)
c) Unit cost should be within 15% of category benchmark
d) Preference for vendors with regional warehouse proximity
e) ESG compliance score required for new contracts from Q3 2026

4. ORDER QUANTITY GUIDELINES
4.1 Standard Orders
Recommended order quantity = (reorder_point × 2.5) - current_stock_level
Minimum order: respect vendor minimum order quantity (MOQ)
Maximum single order: 5,000 cases unless C-Suite approval obtained

4.2 Risk-Based Overrides
High-risk SKUs (governance.risk_tier = high): order up to reorder_point × 3.5
Baby and pharmaceutical categories: maintain 90-day safety stock at all times

5. PROHIBITED ACTIONS
- Split orders to circumvent approval thresholds (order splitting) is a policy violation
- Sole-source procurement above $25,000 requires documented justification
- Backdating purchase orders is prohibited and subject to disciplinary action

6. SUSTAINABILITY REQUIREMENTS
From Q3 2026, all new supplier contracts must include:
- Carbon footprint disclosure
- Recyclable or reduced packaging commitment
- Living wage certification for manufacturing workforce

7. AUDIT AND COMPLIANCE
All AI-generated procurement recommendations are logged with full trace IDs.
Quarterly audits review AI recommendation acceptance rates and outcome accuracy.
